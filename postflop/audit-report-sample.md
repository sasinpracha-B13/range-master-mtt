## Notes for reviewer

### Strong points of the seed

1. **Critical leak coverage** — the 5 most common preflop-raiser postflop leaks (over-c-bet on low-connected, polar big on dry A-high, slowplay top two on dry, range-c-bet small on monotone low, auto-c-bet overpair on dynamic) are all represented as `critical` answers with explicit `commonMistake` explanations.
2. **Range vs nut split** — multiple scenarios show that range advantage and nut advantage are independent (Th8h3h: neutral range / preflop_raiser nut; 7d7s3c: split range / neutral nut), reinforcing the principle that they're separate dimensions.
3. **Difficulty distribution** — heavily weighted to foundation (1) and intermediate (2), which matches the v4.0 goal of building a strong base before introducing advanced spots.
4. **Source confidence** — 90% `consensus_gto`, 10% `expert_judgment`. Zero `experimental`. This means the seed dataset is defensible without hedging.

### Known gaps (intentional, listed for v4.1+ planning)

1. **Concept starvation** for `nut_advantage_shift`, `ip_advantage`, `equity_realization`. These are foundational meta-concepts that deserve dedicated scenarios in v4.1.
2. **Mixed-region scenarios** — only one `mixed_small_check` example. Real GTO mixing is more common than the seed suggests; v4.1 should add 5–8 mixed scenarios with proper frequency data.
3. **Difficulty-5 (expert)** — none. v4.0 is a foundation pass; difficulty-5 spots fit better once the player has cleared 1–4.
4. **Suit texture** — 4 monotone, 8 two-tone, 19 rainbow. The rainbow bias is correct for a foundation pass (cleanest range/nut analysis) but v4.1 should rebalance toward more two-tone and monotone for realism.
5. **High-card class** — 12 low boards, 8 A-high, 5 K-high, only 2 each Q/J/T-high. Q-high and J-high deserve more attention in v4.1 — they're trickier than the high-A boards but easier than connected low.

### Recommended v4.1 expansion targets

Priority order (highest impact first):

1. Add 8–10 Q-high and J-high scenarios (currently underrepresented).
2. Add 5–8 `mixed_small_check` scenarios to demonstrate true mixed strategy.
3. Add dedicated `ip_advantage` and `equity_realization` meta-questions (4–6 scenarios).
4. Add `nut_advantage_shift` examples (turn-card swing) — but this is technically v4.2 (turn module).
5. Rebalance suit texture toward 50% rainbow / 35% two-tone / 15% monotone to match real-world distribution.
6. Add 3–5 difficulty-4 advanced scenarios on the trickier mixed regions.
7. Bring total to 80–100 scenarios for v4.1, then 120–160 for v4.2.
