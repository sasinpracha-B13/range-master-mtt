⚠️ Important: Study Tool Only

Range Master MTT is designed for OFFLINE STUDY of preflop poker ranges. It is NOT a real-time assistance tool.

❌ DO NOT use this app during live poker play:

  • Using reference charts/apps during active hands violates Terms of Service of all major poker platforms
  • Violations can result in permanent account ban + fund confiscation

✅ DO use this app:

  • Before sessions (warm-up drill)
  • After sessions (review mistakes)
  • During free time (focused study)

The goal: memorize ranges so well you don't NEED any reference during play.

Ranges shown are approximations based on published Nash/GTO solutions. Play responsibly.
